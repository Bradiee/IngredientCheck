Terms of Service

1. Liability waiver

IngredientCheck is an information tool only. You consume all food items entirely at your own risk. IngredientCheck makes no medical guarantees about ingredient accuracy. We do not analyze health — we index stated text submitted by manufacturers and restaurants.

2. Data accuracy

IngredientCheck matches your typed search string to the text submitted by retailers. We do not independently test or verify products in a laboratory. Ingredient formulas change. Always read the physical label and consult the establishment manager before consuming if you have a serious allergy or medical condition.

IngredientCheck never labels products as "X-free." We use the language "No X ingredients" or "Processed on a line that handles X." This is intentional and legally important.
